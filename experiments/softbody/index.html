About

This simulation demonstrates how geometric constraints create realistic soft body deformation.

Key Concepts:

  • Structural Constraints: Connect adjacent particles
  • Shear Constraints: Connect diagonal particles
  • Bend Constraints: Connect every second particle
  • Pressure Constraints: Preserve volume (inflation)

The shape emerges from satisfying these constraints iteratively - a direct application of constraint theory!
